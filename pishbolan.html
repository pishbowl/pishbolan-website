si pishbol

I explore the intersection of form, light, and negative space through minimalist photography. My work focuses on finding beauty in simplicity, capturing the essence of subjects through careful composition and a reductive approach.

With a background in design and fine arts, I bring an architect's eye to the photographic medium. Each image is a study in balance, exploring the relationship between presence and absence.

Based in Berlin, my work has been exhibited in galleries across Europe and featured in publications dedicated to contemporary minimalist art and photography.
